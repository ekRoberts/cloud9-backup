About Me

Hello! My name is Emma Roberts and I love people! I grew up in Walla Walla, Washington but I have lived in Utah since college and I never left. I have been a photographer since 2018 and my experience means I can take care of the details so your photo shoot is about you having fun! I love the mountains and capturing beautiful moments in them is one of my favorite parts of being a photographer. However, my main passion is capturing my subjects in their natural environment. My goal is to help you be comfortable in front of the camera so that your pictures reflect who you truly are. My black and white style allows you to be bare in the photo and showcases the true moment without being over saturated. If you are looking for a photo shoot where the subject is focus my style is for you.

My main types of shoots include:

  • Portraits
  • Weddings
  • Engagements
  • Family Photos

If you have an idea you want to bring to life, reach out! I love being a part of the creative process and would love to work with you.

Let's capture moments together!
